🛡️ Shield Math — Don't Skip This

A 24-hour shield costs only 4,800 gems. Healing 500k troops costs approximately ~50,000 gems worth of speedups and resources. The math wins every time. Shield up.

Final Reminder

Winning KvK is far more valuable than personal stats. Don't chase solo kills. Work with your alliance, follow rally calls, and fight for Castles and Turrets together. A coordinated legion always outperforms a kingdom of lone wolves. ✌️
