🎯 Step-by-Step Flow

Follow this order every event. Using items wrong or in the wrong mode wastes your limited attempts.

1
Before Your First Cast
Passes · Mail · Free Items
✅ Do First
💳Buy your Fishing Pass (Pro or Master) if spending — the score multiplier applies to ALL future catches. Buying late = lost points.
📬Check your in-game mail for free items.
📦Open all event chests — collect your Ocean Prospector Map, Reel, Ocean Scanner and Lantern before casting.
❌ Don't
🚫Cast your first line without checking mail first — you'll waste the boosted first cast.
🚫Buy passes mid-event — every previous catch missed the multiplier.
2
🗺️ Use Your Ocean Prospector First
Your most powerful single cast
✅ What It Does
⭐Temporarily maxes your Line, Hook and Sinker to Level 10 for that single cast — even if your gear is at Level 1.
💰Gives you a massive points head-start to immediately upgrade your real gear.
📦Only place to find rare chests and rare mermaids — not in regular fishing.
💡 Key Notes
🚫Horn of the Tides is WASTED in Ocean Prospector — save it for regular fishing only.
🏆Use Treasure Charts here carefully — this is where Ocean Chests with rare main-game items appear.
⚙️
Upgrade Your Gear — Right Order
Hook → Line → Sinker
✅ Priority Order
🪝Hook first — more fish per cast. More hooks = more points per run.
🧵Line second — reach deeper depth where rarer, higher-star fish live.
⚙️Sinker last — sets your starting depth. Least urgent upgrade early.
💡 Going Deep
↔️While descending, hug the left or right edge of the screen to avoid snagging fish too early.
🎯When you decide to catch — aim for the fish's head for best catch rate, especially on rare fish.
🎣
Regular Fishing — What to Grab
Points · Mermaids · Chests
✅ Always Grab
✨All glowing fish — sparkling fish are always worth more. Prioritize 2-star and 3-star. Never skip them.
🧜Distressed mermaids (wrapped in seaweed) — rescue for a 5–10% score multiplier. Always take them.
🎺Horn of the Tides — use here only to summon specific rare fish or mermaids at target depths.
❌ Skip / Leave
🚫1-star common fish when 2-star fish are in range — don't fill your hook with junk early.
🚫Healthy (unwrapped) mermaids — they give no benefit, don't waste hook space.
🚫Bad cast? Hit pause → retreat and try again. See the P-R-P tab for full technique.
⚠️ Fishing Voucher timing: Vouchers give a 1.5x bonus to both Fishing Tokens and Points. Use them at the very end of the event when your gear is fully maxed — using them early on weak gear is one of the most common mistakes.
🎣

The Golden Rule

Ocean Prospector first for the gear boost. Horn of Tides in regular fishing only — never in Prospector. Grab every glowing fish and distressed mermaid. Use your Fishing Voucher last when gear is maxed. And if a cast goes wrong — P-R-P. Patience wins the leaderboard. ✌️

🔁 The P-R-P Technique

Community-discovered method for resetting bad casts without wasting your attempts or consumables.

Pause → Retreat → Play Again

Originally spotted in YouTube Shorts. Hit pause mid-cast, retreat, and try again — your attempt and all pre-cast consumables are saved. This is the key to efficient mermaid hunting and score maximizing.

🎺Horn of Tides used before starting — and it CARRIES OVER to the next try
🔦Reel, Ocean Scanner and Lantern
📦Treasure item layouts — they refresh every P-R-P, helping you find items you're missing
⚠️ What P-R-P Can't Do
🚫You cannot change or swap consumables after starting — plan your Horn, Reel, Scanner before your first cast.
🚫You must finish or commit to the current challenge before modifying anything again.

🧜 Mermaid Hunting Guide

Personal experiments by Sensei Norman — tested over 3 fishing events. The 100m trick is how Princess Nairo was finally caught.

🧜
The Depth Theory — Tested
What 2 days of P-R-P experiments revealed

Summoning a fish using Horn of Tides at a specific depth appears to suppress mermaid spawns at that same depth. Mermaids get pushed to appear at other depths instead. This behaviour was observed repeatedly over 2 days of controlled P-R-P testing.

0m (surface)550m (max depth — no mermaids ever)

✅ 200–500m — mermaids spawn freely here
❌ 550m
🎯 The 100m trick: Summon any fish using Horn of Tides at 100m depth only. This suppresses mermaids near 100m and pushes them to spawn at 200–500m — exactly where rare mermaids like Princess Nairo live. Use P-R-P to keep retrying until she appears.

Full Mermaid Hunting Strategy
How Norman completed the full collection
✅ The Method
1️⃣Set Horn of Tides to summon any fish at 100m depth only.
2️⃣Start the cast — if the target mermaid doesn't appear, P-R-P immediately.
3️⃣Repeat. The Horn effect carries over with P-R-P so you don't waste one per attempt.
4️⃣When the rare mermaid appears in a good depth position — commit and go deep.
⏳ Patience Required
📅Princess Nairo took 2 full days of P-R-P attempts to appear.
🎲Sometimes no mermaid appears even with the method — RNG is still a factor.
✅It took 3 full fishing events to complete the entire mermaid collection. Don't panic if it takes time.
