Always confirm a Relic Collector is in position before any assault launches. A building captured without relic collection is half a win.

📋 Core Team Rules

Three things every player must follow, no exceptions.

1

Show Up If You Register

Matchmaking is based on registered players. If you sign up and don't show, the whole team fights short-handed and everyone else has a harder battle. Only register if you can actually participate.

2

Don't Go Solo Without Good Reason

If you're zeroed and pulled off the field, your team fights 12 minutes without you. Avoid unnecessary risks — stay with the team and don't pick fights you can't win alone.

3

Always Call and Join Rallies

Hero widgets activate on rallies — even if nobody joins, launching one still powers your widgets and makes your attack far stronger than a direct hit. Always rally. Always join rallies.

🔥 Free-for-All Players

Not locked into a zone? Here's how you can still make a real difference on your own terms.

💎

Relic Collector

Position near contested buildings before the attack lands. Relics dropped on occupation are a massive team boost — this role directly feeds your legion's power.

Critical Role
⚔️

Solo Hunter

High battle IQ and strong stats let you break through even against big odds — zero enemy stragglers and remove threats before they reinforce key objectives.

Aggressive
🏰

Abbey Flipper

Capture abandoned Abbeys that enemies have left idle. Even low-point buildings accumulate over a long match, and flipping them forces enemies to split focus.

Strategic
🛡️

Lone Defender

Sit on a Sanctum or Reformation the main force can't spare defenders for. Being the quiet anchor of a captured building is an underrated contribution.

Defensive
🔔

Bell Tower Keeper

Hold the Bell Tower solo. Its passive capture-speed buff gives your whole team an invisible edge during every push on Middle and Sanctums.

Utility
🧹

Mercenary Denial

Camp the enemy Mercenary and recapture it whenever it goes idle. Stopping its constant tick damage to our Middle is a quiet but decisive play.

Disruptive
📣

Rally Joiner

Fill rally slots — even if you're not the strongest attacker. Your presence unlocks hero widget buffs for the rally leader and makes every hit harder.

Team Support
🚑

Heal & Return

Don't burn all speedups to stay active. Heal, recover your troops, and come back at full strength. A fresh army in the final minutes wins fights.

Long Game
⚔️

Final Reminder

Always confirm a Relic Collector is in position before any building assault launches. Call it in the chat if you're filling the role. One missed relic drop after a hard-fought capture can cost the team thousands of points. Hold the Middle. Win as one. ✌️
