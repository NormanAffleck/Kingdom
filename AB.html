🎯 The Strategy

Why we're splitting — and how it gives everyone a better shot at Top 5.

🥇
Top 5 Wins — Regardless of Alliance Result
Personal ranking is independent from alliance outcome

The best rewards go to the top 5 ranked players in their group, even if their alliance loses the brawl overall. That means placement, not just participation, is what matters most.

🔀
Splitting into Small Groups
3–5 players per farm alliance

Instead of 100 players competing for the same 5 spots in EON, we temporarily move into smaller, less active farm alliances. With only 3–5 of us per group, everyone has a dramatically better chance of landing Top 5.

🛡️
KvK Stays the Priority
Save your resources — this is a side play

This is a smart resource-efficient play. We're not burning our reserves here — KvK remains our main focus. Alliance Brawl is a bonus opportunity to earn great rewards without compromising our war effort.

⚠️
Alliance Ranking Threshold: To receive Alliance Ranking Rewards, your alliance must reach 20 million total points. That's roughly 4 million points per member — very achievable with this strategy.
📅 Full Schedule

Day-by-day breakdown of what to do and when.

PreDay
📋 Pre-Day 1 — Preparation Important
  • Form your team for Alliance Brawl.
  • Teams assigned based on Kingdom of Power Prep Phase rankings (KvK ranking rewards).
  • Move to your designated alliance before UTC 23:00.
💡 Send resource gatherers out before UTC 16:00 so they're ready to be recalled on Day 1 for instant gathering points.
1Day
🐻 Day 1 — Bear Hunt + Gathering Bear Hunt Gathering Caravans
  • Send your Caravans and raid enemy caravans.
  • Use Gold (Mythic) Caravans whenever possible for both sending and attacking.
  • Send caravans as late as possible — most players burn their attacks right after reset, making late caravans much safer.
  • Recall all gatherers and claim gathering points.
🐻 Bear 1 — UTC 18:30 (Return to EON)
🐻 Bear 2 — UTC 00:30 (Return to EON)
💡 After each Bear Hunt, return to your assigned alliance.
📌 Tip for Day 2: Save your UTC 16:00 Intel. Activate Pan's extra Intel after UTC 16:00.
2Day
🔍 Day 2 — Intel Day Intel Caravans
  • Send and raid caravans.
  • Complete all available Intels.
  • With your saved UTC 16:00 Intels, daily reset Intels, and Pan's extra Intels from Day 1 & Day 2 — you should easily reach the daily point cap. Nothing else is required. 🙂
📌 Tip for Day 3: Send gatherers before UTC 16:00.
3Day
🐻 Day 3 — Bear Hunt + Gathering Bear Hunt Gathering Caravans
  • Send and raid caravans.
  • Claim gathering points.
  • Return to EON for both Bear Hunts.
📌 Tip for Day 4: Save your UTC 16:00 Intel. Activate Pan's extra Intel after UTC 16:00.
4Day
🔍 Day 4 — Intel Day Intel Caravans
  • Send and raid caravans.
  • Complete all Intels.
📌 Tip for Day 5: Sorry… no special tip today. 😄
5Day
🦁 Day 5 — Bear Hunt + Beast Slaying Bear Hunt Beasts Caravans
  • Send and raid caravans.
  • Focus on killing Beasts.
  • Each Beast also gives an extra Champion's Way reward box.
  • Try to split your Ham usage between Day 5 and Day 6.
6Day
🌩️ Day 6 — Terror Day Terrors Caravans
  • Send and raid caravans.
  • Rally Terrors as much as possible.
  • Each Terror defeated rewards additional speedups.
  • No Gathering or Intel missions today.
⚠️

Final Reminder

To receive Alliance Ranking Rewards, your alliance must reach 20 million total points. That means each member should average roughly 4 million points. With this strategy, reaching that target should be very achievable — while giving everyone a much better shot at Top 5 personal rewards. Cheers! ✌️
