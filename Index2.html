Final Reflection

What this course changed

Most Interesting

Branch & Bound

The idea that you can systematically prune an entire solution space by proving certain branches can't beat your best answer — without testing every possibility — felt like a superpower once it clicked.

Most Challenging

The Pizza Optimizer

Binary variables sound simple — 0 or 1 — but formulating the model correctly, running sensitivity analysis, and understanding why the greedy approach fails taught me more than any lecture.

Most Valuable

Thinking in Models

The real skill isn't solving the math — it's translating a messy real-world problem into a clean model with sets, parameters, decision variables, and constraints. That framing works everywhere.

How My Understanding Changed

Everything is an optimization

Before: I made decisions intuitively. After: I catch myself identifying objectives, constraints, and tradeoffs automatically. Star Wars, pizza, university budgets — they're all the same structure underneath.
